Crypto Wallet is a browser-based wallet for the Solana blockchain. It generates a brand-new ed25519 keypair in your browser, stores it for the session, and gives you a dashboard to view balances, send SOL, and swap tokens.

By default the app connects to **Solana devnet**, so you can experiment with real on-chain transactions without spending real funds.

## What you can do

- **Create a wallet** — generate a fresh ed25519 keypair, view your public address, reveal your private key, and download a JSON backup.
- **Fund your wallet** — request a devnet airdrop from the Solana faucet to start transacting.
- **Manage SOL** — check your balance, view transaction history, and send SOL to any address.
- **Swap tokens** — get on-chain quotes and trade SOL for USDC using Orca's devnet pools.

## How it works

The wallet runs entirely client-side. Keys are generated in your browser with Solana's `Keypair.generate()` and held in React context for the lifetime of the session — nothing is sent to a server. The dashboard reads the in-memory keypair to fetch your balance, sign transfers, and execute swaps against the configured RPC endpoint.

<Warning>
  This wallet is built for learning and demonstration on devnet. Do not use it to hold mainnet funds or store keys you cannot afford to lose.
</Warning>
